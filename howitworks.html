Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use the system?
+
Not at all. We build everything, configure everything, and train your team from scratch. If you can send a text message, you can use what we build for you.
How long does it take to get everything running?
+
Most clients have a live website and active automations within 2 to 3 weeks of signing on. We move fast because we know your business cannot afford to wait.
What is Go High Level and do I need to learn it all?
+
Go High Level is the CRM platform that powers your automations, pipelines, and communications. We set it up completely and train you on exactly the parts you use day-to-day. Nothing overwhelming.
What if I want to cancel?
+
Month-to-month means month-to-month. No contracts, no lock-in. If it is not working for you, cancel any time. We would rather earn your business every month than trap it.
Do you work with businesses outside Pensacola?
+
Yes. While we are based in Pensacola and love working with local businesses, everything we do is remote-friendly. We work with small businesses all across the US.
